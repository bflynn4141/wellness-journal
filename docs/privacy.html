Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 27, 2024

Overview

Wellness Journal is a personal, open-source CLI application designed for individual use. This privacy policy explains how the application handles your data.

Data Collection

Wellness Journal accesses the following data from connected services:

  • Whoop: Recovery scores, HRV, sleep data, and strain metrics
  • Google Calendar: Event titles, times, and durations for the current day

Data Storage

All data is stored locally on your device. Specifically:

  • Journal entries are stored in a SQLite database in ~/.wellness-journal/
  • OAuth tokens are encrypted and stored locally
  • Daily notes are saved as Markdown files in your Obsidian vault

Data Sharing

Wellness Journal does not:

  • Send your data to any external servers (except to fetch from Whoop/Google)
  • Share your data with third parties
  • Use your data for analytics or advertising
  • Store any data outside your local machine

Third-Party Services

When you connect Whoop or Google Calendar, you authorize the application to access your data from those services. Their respective privacy policies apply:

  • Whoop Privacy Policy
  • Google Privacy Policy

Data Deletion

To delete all your data:

  • Remove the ~/.wellness-journal/ directory
  • Delete the Obsidian daily notes folder
  • Revoke access in your Whoop and Google account settings

Open Source

This application is open source. You can review the code to verify these privacy practices at github.com/bflynn4141/wellness-journal.

Contact

For questions about this privacy policy, please open an issue on the GitHub repository.
